OpenHI Kit

Event-based hyperspectral microscope kit.

Event-based hyperspectral microscope kit with PCBs, 3D-printed parts, motors, LED illumination, grating, and a 4f relay for high-speed spectral imaging. Open hardware and software for researchers, educators, and innovators to explore customizable microscopy.

Designed for lab benches and classrooms, the kit pairs open hardware with extensible software so you can prototype new spectral experiments faster.

* Note: Camera, tube lens, and optics table not included.
